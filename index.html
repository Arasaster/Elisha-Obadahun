About Me

Hey there, I'm Elisha Excel Obadahun

I'm a passionate graphic designer and web developer currently studying Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at the Airforce Institute of Technology in Kaduna, Nigeria.

My creative journey began during the COVID-19 lockdown, where I taught myself design and development skills through online resources. What started as a way to pass time transformed into my passion and purpose.

I believe in becoming part of your success story, and you becoming part of mine. When I take on a project, I'm not just designing a logo or building a website—I'm helping craft your legacy.
